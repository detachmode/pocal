Pocal is a new Calendar App for Windows Phone 8.1 with a innovative user experience.
It combines an agenda view with a day view. That way you can easiliy get a overview over your next appointments. But if you want to get a more detailed view over one day , or want to add an appointment at a specific time, you can just tap on a day or an appointment and a little day view window will pop up. This day view is similiar to the day view in the native Windows Phone calendar.
It also has a month view to navigate to a specific date and an overview view to see more appointments at one glance. To enter the Overview you can simple swipe left or right in the agenda view.

 Feature list:
 • Agenda view combined with a day view.
 • An Overview view to see more appointments at one glance. 
 • Minimalistic design 
 • Fast app launch
 • Design follows the Windows Phone guidelines.
 • Smart usage of gestures.
